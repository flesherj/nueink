## Why "Idea Theft" Is a Myth

### Reason 1: Ideas Are Worthless Without Execution

**The harsh truth:**
- Your idea isn't unique (sorry!)
- Someone else has probably thought of it
- What matters is WHO SHIPS FIRST
- And who EXECUTES BEST

**Example:**
- Facebook wasn't first social network (Friendster, MySpace)
- Google wasn't first search engine (Yahoo, AltaVista)
- iPhone wasn't first smartphone (BlackBerry, Palm)

**They won on EXECUTION, not ideas.**

---

### Reason 2: Most People Won't Build It

**Even if someone loves your idea:**

**90% won't start:**
- "Great idea! Someone should build that"
- "I'd use that if it existed"
- "Sounds hard, good luck!"

**9% will start, then quit:**
- Week 1: "I'm building this!"
- Week 2: "This is harder than I thought..."
- Week 3: "Lost motivation"
- Week 4: Abandoned

**1% will actually ship:**
- But by then, you're already ahead
- You have users, feedback, iteration
- You're on v10, they're on v1

**The internet is LITTERED with abandoned projects.**
