## Risks & Mitigation

### Technical Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| YNAB API changes | Low | High | Monitor API, quick updates, communicate with users |
| AWS costs spike | Low | Medium | Set billing alerts, optimize early |
| Scaling issues | Medium | Medium | Use serverless, scales automatically |
| Security breach | Low | High | AWS IAM best practices, encrypt data, regular audits |

### Business Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |

| Low adoption | Medium | Medium | Start as personal tool, low pressure |

| User churn | Medium | Medium | Focus on quality, great support, regular updates |

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
